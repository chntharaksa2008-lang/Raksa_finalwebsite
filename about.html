About Tube Coffee

Tube Coffee Cambodia serves delicious coffee and drinks in a cozy atmosphere.

The story began with 3 Cambodian coffee-lover youths, with coffee brand establishment dream that truly represents Khmer people materializing in 2017 as the first store — a simple but attractive Tube Coffee Kdan Two — being inaugurated. With exceptional emphasis on fast service, affordable price tag, and high-quality products, TUBE COFFEE have experienced radical transformation to the leading Khmer people favorite coffee and food chain in Cambodia, indicated by the ever-growing expansion clocking at 43 stores, 8 of which are TUBE COFFEE .
